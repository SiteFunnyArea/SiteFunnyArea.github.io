NoPingBot



Commands

The set prefix for the bot is "no!". You cannot change this as of right now.

no!add [user id] - Adds a user to your no-ping list.
no!remove [user id] - Removes a user from your no-ping list.
no!list - Lists your no-ping list.


Getting User IDs

This bot doesn't support pinging users to add to your no-ping list.
To add users to your no-ping list, you have to use their User ID. This is how you get it.

1. Go in to your User Settings.
2. Find "Advanced" in the sidebar of your user settings.
3. One of the options should say "Developer Mode". If it is disabled, enable it by clicking the checkbox next to the setting.
4. Exit your User Settings.
5. Either right-click the user you want to add to your list, or click of their user profile.
6. If you right-clicked, click Copy ID. If you clicked on their profile, click the three dots at the top right corner of their profile, and click "Copy ID".


After you do this, you will have the persons User ID.
